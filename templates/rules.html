Tournament Rules

Everything you need to know about participating in SportSpot tournaments.

General Rules

  • All players must be registered on SportSpot with complete profiles.
  • Tournament registration must be completed before the specified deadline.
  • Players can only be registered with one team per tournament.
  • All matches will be played according to the official tournament schedule.

Team Formation

  • Teams must have a minimum of 11 players and maximum of 15 players.
  • Each team must designate a captain who will be responsible for team management.
  • Team rosters must be finalized before the tournament begins.
  • Player transfers between teams are not allowed during the tournament.

Match Rules

  • Matches will consist of specified overs as per tournament format.
  • Teams must arrive at least 30 minutes before scheduled match time.
  • Match officials' decisions are final and must be respected.
  • Points will be awarded according to tournament guidelines.

Code of Conduct

  • Players must maintain sportsmanship and respect towards all participants.
  • Any form of discrimination or harassment will not be tolerated.
  • Violations of the code of conduct may result in penalties or disqualification.
  • Teams are responsible for their supporters' behavior.

Frequently Asked Questions

Create an account on SportSpot, navigate to the tournament page, and click on "Register Team". Follow the instructions to complete your team registration.
In case of postponement, teams will be notified through the platform and email. The rescheduled match details will be updated on the tournament schedule.
Disputes should be reported to tournament officials through the platform. A committee will review and make decisions based on tournament rules and regulations.

Still Have Questions?

If you have any questions about the rules or regulations, please reach out to the tournament organizers directly through your event page.
